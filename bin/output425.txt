<TITLE>STERLING SOFTWARE &lt;SSW> BUYS BACK SHARES</TITLE>
<DATELINE>    DALLAS, Oct 20 - </DATELINE><BODY>Sterling Software Inc said it has bought
back 394,100 shares of its stock in the open market since
October 8.
    "In a market full of sellers we're a confident buyer and
we'll continue purchasing shares," president Sterling Williams
said.
