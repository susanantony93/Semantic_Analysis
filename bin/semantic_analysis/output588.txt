<TITLE>VIACOM &lt;VIA> EXECUTIVE RESIGNS</TITLE>
<DATELINE>    NEW YORK, Oct 20 - </DATELINE><BODY>Viacom Inc said Gordon E. Belt has
resigned as vice president, chief financial officer of Viacom
Interntional Inc, effective Nov 13, 1987.
    The company said a successor will be announced shortly.
    Viacom said Belt has not announced immediate plans after
his resignation.
